Mission 2: Learn 5 Words Only
Forget everything else. Just these 5. That's enough.
✓
▼

If you remember nothing else from investing school, just know these 5 words. You'll sound smarter than 90% of people at the next family dinner.

Profit
Money left after all costs. The real number that matters.
Dividend
Cash paid to you just for holding the stock. Like rental income.
PE Ratio
Is this stock expensive? Low PE = cheaper. High PE = pricier.
Revenue
Total sales money coming in — before any costs.
Portfolio
All your stocks combined. Your total investment collection.
